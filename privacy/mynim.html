Privacy Policy — Mynim

Health Data Notice: Health-related information in Mynim is NEVER sold, NEVER shared with insurers or employers, and NEVER used for advertising.

1. Introduction

Mynim is published by Chris Shepard through Herding Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how Mynim collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. This policy is effective March 2026.

2. What We Collect

Mynim may collect account information, dose logs, injection site history, weight and wellness entries, and limited usage analytics needed to maintain app reliability. Subscription and payment data are processed by Apple and not directly stored as payment card records by Herding Labs.

3. How We Use It

Collected information is used only to provide dose tracking, history views, reminders, product stability, account support, and app improvements requested by users.

4. AI and Your Photos

Mynim does not require photo-based AI processing as a core feature. If optional AI features are introduced, this section will be updated to describe the provider, purpose, and retention terms before those features are enabled.

5. Third-Party Services

Mynim uses Apple StoreKit for subscriptions and may rely on infrastructure vendors for secure hosting and analytics. Third-party services are contractually limited to providing app operations and cannot use data for unrelated marketing.

6. Data Storage and Security

Herding Labs applies reasonable safeguards including encrypted transport, access controls, and restricted operational access. While no method is perfect, security and retention practices are reviewed as the product evolves.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data by contacting support. You can also remove local app data and cancel subscriptions through your Apple account settings at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Mynim is intended for users age 13 and older and is not knowingly directed to children under 13. If such data is identified, Herding Labs will remove it within a reasonable time.

9. Changes to This Policy

Policy updates may occur to reflect legal, technical, or product changes. Revised versions will include an updated effective date and additional notice when required.

10. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to support@herdinglabs.com.
