Privacy Policy - Grayne

Effective date: March 2026. Grayne is published by Herding Labs. This policy explains what information Grayne processes and why.

1. The short version

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for targeted advertising.
  • We process your photos and inputs to generate outfit-related results and to make the app work.
  • You can request export or deletion by emailing support@herdinglabs.com.

2. Information Grayne may collect or process

Depending on how you use the app, Grayne may process:

  • Wardrobe photos and item details you add (for example: shirts, shoes, outerwear).
  • Account and device identifiers needed to keep the app working (for example: login identifiers, purchase/subscription status via Apple).
  • Usage and diagnostics such as crash logs or performance events (used to improve reliability).
  • Weather-related context that may include general location information if you enable location access, so the app can give weather-aware suggestions.

Payments: subscriptions and billing are handled by Apple. Herding Labs does not receive your full payment card details.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Generate outfit suggestions and run core features you request.
  • Personalize your experience (for example: your closet, preferences, and history).
  • Maintain subscriptions and entitlements through Apple.
  • Diagnose bugs, prevent abuse, and improve app stability.
  • Respond to support requests.

4. AI processing (photos and text)

Some Grayne features use AI to analyze photos (like clothing item images) or generate suggestions. When you use those features, relevant inputs may be sent securely to AI providers (for example, Google Gemini) for processing. The purpose is to generate the result you requested (like an item description or an outfit suggestion).

5. Sharing and third parties

Grayne uses third-party services to operate, including:

  • Apple (StoreKit) for subscriptions and billing.
  • AI providers (for example, Google Gemini) when you use AI-powered features.

These providers process data under their own terms and security practices. We share only what is needed for the feature to function.

6. Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. Retention can vary based on feature usage, storage settings, and your support requests.

7. Your choices and rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data by emailing support@herdinglabs.com. You can also stop using Grayne at any time by removing the app and managing subscriptions through your Apple account settings.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No system is perfectly secure, but we take security seriously and improve controls as the product evolves.

9. Childrens privacy

Grayne is intended for users age 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date and, when appropriate, notice in the app or on this site.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@herdinglabs.com.
