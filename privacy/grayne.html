Privacy Policy — Grayne

1. Introduction

Grayne is published by Chris Shepard through Herding Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how Grayne collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app. This policy is effective March 2026 and applies to all users of Grayne.

2. What We Collect

Grayne may collect photos you provide of clothing items, account information such as your email or Apple account identifier, app usage data such as feature interactions, and general location data used to deliver weather-aware outfit suggestions. Subscription and payment processing are handled by Apple; Herding Labs does not receive full payment card details.

3. How We Use It

Information is used to operate Grayne, generate outfit suggestions, personalize your experience, maintain account access, improve product quality, diagnose bugs, and provide support responses.

4. AI and Your Photos

To generate outfit recommendations, photos may be sent securely to Google Gemini AI services for processing. Photos are processed for the requested feature and are not retained long-term by Herding Labs beyond operational needs for generating results and maintaining app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

Grayne uses third-party providers including Google Gemini for AI processing and Apple StoreKit for subscriptions and billing. These providers process data under their own privacy terms and security practices.

6. Data Storage and Security

Herding Labs uses reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No system is perfectly secure, but security controls are reviewed and improved regularly.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations and legitimate operational requirements. You may also discontinue use of Grayne at any time by removing the app and canceling your subscription through Apple.

8. Children's Privacy

Grayne is intended for users age 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. If data from a child under 13 is discovered, Herding Labs will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated effective date and, when appropriate, in-app notice.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@herdinglabs.com.
