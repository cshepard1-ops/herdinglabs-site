ABOUT

I'm Chris Shepard. I build apps I'd actually use.

Herding Labs is a one-person studio. No investors, no board, no product committee. Just me, figuring out what's missing from my phone and then building it.

The name comes from "herding cats," because ideas are chaos. The "labs" part is the discipline of turning that chaos into something real. The logo is a magnet pulling scattered dots into focus. That's basically my job description.

I care about craft. Clean design, honest pricing, software that respects your time. If I ship something, it's because I'd pay for it myself.

Based in Northern California. Building in public when I remember to.
